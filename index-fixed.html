As the world becomes a more digital place,
we cannot forget about the human connection.

The way of communication has experienced periods of letters, radios, telephones and social media platforms. It's undoubtedly that the usage of the internet has been the most helpful way of remaining connected nowadays.

Videoconferencing, although has been a well-developed technology for quite some time now, is just now being realized as a powerful communication tool.

The act of trying to lie on a sofa, holding the phone up high for the best angle, eventually moving closer to the router for a stronger signal has become the new daily workout for everyone these days!



Doctors On Call



Doctors are now able to consult patients through their phone.

While many people may argue that this is not as effective as an in-person appointment, it has been very beneficial in rural or understaffed areas.



Help To The Deaf Community



Video calling has been very beneficial for individuals having hearing difficulties.

Unlike traditional phone calls, the deaf are able to speak in sign language to family members, friends and even colleagues.



Families Reunited



Cheaper than regular phone calls and arguably more personal, video calls allow people to watch their grandkids grow up and see their cousin’s new apartment from the comfort of their own home.

It’s redefined distance and geography making it easier for people to stay in touch.
